About

A finance leader who builds the infrastructure — the models, the controls, the reporting rhythm — that lets a bank or a business make decisions with confidence.

I’m a Senior FP&A Manager with 15+ years of experience across tier-1 international banks and Egypt’s first digital-native bank. As the founding finance leader of onebank (Misr Digital Innovation), I built the entire FP&A function from scratch — long-term financial modeling, capital adequacy planning, stress-testing frameworks, and multi-scenario forecasting for a greenfield institution.

I served as the primary finance counterpart across two independent Big4-led engagements: a strategic equity transaction review and a full business model validation — both approved. My work spans ALCO and treasury performance, MIS automation, core banking transformation, and ERP implementation.

Based in Cairo, fluent in Arabic and English, and currently exploring senior FP&A opportunities across the Gulf, Europe, and Asia — alongside freelance financial modeling and fractional CFO engagements.
